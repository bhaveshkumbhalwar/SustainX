🌱 Waste Awareness

♻️

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

The 3Rs are the foundation of sustainable waste management. Reduce consumption first, reuse items wherever possible, and recycle materials to minimize landfill waste.

SDG Goal 12
🌊

Plastic Pollution Crisis

Over 8 million tonnes of plastic enter our oceans each year. Switching to reusable bottles, bags, and containers can significantly reduce campus plastic waste.

SDG Goal 14
🍎

Food Waste Matters

Approximately 1/3 of all food produced globally is wasted. Take only what you can eat in the cafeteria, and use the food waste bins for proper composting.

SDG Goal 2
⚡

E-Waste Responsibility

Electronic waste contains hazardous materials like lead and mercury. Use designated e-waste collection points on campus to dispose of old electronics safely.
